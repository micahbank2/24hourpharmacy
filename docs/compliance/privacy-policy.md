# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: March 2026*

24HourPharmacy.com is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit this site, how it is used, and what choices you have. Please read this policy in full before using 24HourPharmacy.com.

---

## Information We Collect

**24HourPharmacy.com does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information directly.**

We do not ask for your name, email address, home address, phone number, or health information. We do not build user profiles. We do not sell personal data.

What we do collect is anonymous, aggregated usage data through the third-party analytics and tracking tools described below. This data helps us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

---

## Analytics and Tracking

We use the following analytics and tracking tools. Each is described in detail so you know exactly what is collected and by whom.

### Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

24HourPharmacy.com uses Google Analytics 4, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. GA4 collects the following types of anonymous data:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Referring website or search query that brought you to the site
- Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop), operating system, and browser
- Approximate geographic location at the city level (not precise GPS location)
- Events triggered during your visit (e.g., clicking the pharmacy finder, downloading a discount card)

GA4 uses first-party cookies (stored on your device under the 24hourpharmacy.com domain) to track sessions and recognize returning visitors. No personally identifiable information is sent to Google Analytics.

Data collected by GA4 is processed by Google LLC and subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You can opt out of GA4 tracking using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Microsoft Clarity

24HourPharmacy.com uses Microsoft Clarity, a behavioral analytics tool provided by Microsoft Corporation. Clarity records anonymous session replays and generates heatmaps that show aggregate patterns of user interaction — where visitors click, scroll, and tap on pages.

Clarity does not capture passwords, payment information, or other sensitive form inputs. Session replays are anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Data processed by Clarity is subject to [Microsoft's Privacy Policy](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement). Clarity data helps us identify usability issues and improve the pharmacy finder experience.

### CookieYes

24HourPharmacy.com uses CookieYes to manage cookie consent and compliance with GDPR and CCPA requirements. CookieYes displays the cookie consent banner when you first visit the site and records your consent preferences.

CookieYes stores a small cookie on your device that remembers your consent choices. This is a "strictly necessary" cookie and is set regardless of your consent to other cookie categories — it is required for the consent mechanism itself to function.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Manage Preferences" link in the site footer. Your choices will be respected for future visits.

## Affiliate Link Tracking

When you click an affiliate link on 24HourPharmacy.com — these links use `/go/` URLs managed through ThirstyAffiliates — you are redirected to a partner's website. At that point, the affiliate partner may set cookies on your device to track the referral and attribute any resulting purchase or sign-up to 24HourPharmacy.com.

These affiliate tracking cookies are governed by the privacy policies of the respective partners (e.g., GoodRx, Amazon, CJ Affiliate, Impact). 24HourPharmacy.com is not responsible for the data practices of affiliate partners after you leave this site.

## Google Maps and Places API

The pharmacy finder widget on 24HourPharmacy.com uses Google Maps Platform, including the Places API and the Maps JavaScript API. When you use the pharmacy finder:

- You may be prompted to share your device location. This is optional — you may also enter a zip code or city manually.
- If you consent to share your location, Google's geolocation services are used to determine your coordinates. This data is processed by Google LLC and subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- 24HourPharmacy.com does not store your location data. Searches are processed in your browser and in the Google Maps API, not on our servers.

## Your Rights

**California Residents (CCPA):** Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, California residents have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected about them
- Request deletion of personal information
- Opt out of the "sale" of personal information

24HourPharmacy.com does not sell personal information in the traditional sense. However, the use of third-party advertising cookies (affiliate tracking, GA4 advertising features) may constitute a "sale" under the broad definition in CCPA. You can opt out of these cookies using the "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" option in the CookieYes banner, accessible via the "Manage Preferences" link in the site footer.

**All Users:** You may manage cookie preferences using the CookieYes banner. You may opt out of GA4 data collection using the Google Analytics browser add-on. You may disable cookies entirely in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.
