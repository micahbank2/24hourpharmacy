# Affiliate Disclosure

*Last Updated: March 2026*

24HourPharmacy.com is committed to transparency about how this site operates and generates revenue. Please read this disclosure carefully so you understand the nature of the content and links on this site.

## How We Make Money

24HourPharmacy.com is a free resource for consumers. We do not charge for access to the pharmacy finder, discount card information, or any other content on this site. To sustain this resource and continue providing free tools to users, 24HourPharmacy.com participates in affiliate marketing programs.

When you click certain links on this site and then make a purchase, sign up for a service, or complete another qualifying action, 24HourPharmacy.com may receive a commission from the partner company. **This does not affect the price you pay.** You will not pay more by clicking an affiliate link on this site — in many cases, you will pay less because we link directly to savings programs.

Affiliate commissions are how we keep this site free and funded. We appreciate your support.

## Affiliate Relationships

24HourPharmacy.com maintains affiliate partnerships with companies in the following categories. These relationships may generate compensation when you take action through our links:

**Prescription Discount Card Providers**
We partner with discount card networks that negotiate lower drug prices at pharmacies nationwide. Partners include programs like GoodRx, SingleCare, and RxSaver. When you download a discount card or claim a savings coupon through a link on this site, we may earn a referral fee.

**Pharmacy Chains**
We may maintain affiliate relationships with national pharmacy chains — including CVS, Walgreens, and Walmart — through affiliate networks such as CJ Affiliate. These relationships allow us to earn commissions on qualifying online pharmacy purchases.

**Online Pharmacies and Mail-Order Services**
We participate in the Amazon Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites. This includes Amazon Pharmacy. Purchases made through Amazon Pharmacy links may earn 24HourPharmacy.com a commission.

**Telehealth Services**
We maintain affiliate relationships with telehealth providers, including platforms such as Hims and Ro, through affiliate networks like Impact. When you sign up for or purchase from a telehealth provider through a link on our site, we may earn a commission.

**Health Insurance Marketplaces**
We may earn referral fees through health insurance comparison and enrollment services, including GoHealth and eHealth, managed through CJ Affiliate and similar networks.

We periodically update our affiliate partnerships. Not every company or service mentioned on 24HourPharmacy.com is an affiliate partner — we also link to resources without compensation when they are genuinely useful to our readers.

## FTC Compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials, as set forth in 16 CFR Part 255: "Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising."

The FTC requires that websites disclose any material connection between the publisher and companies whose products or services are recommended or linked. A material connection exists when there is a financial relationship, such as an affiliate commission, that could influence what a publisher recommends.

24HourPharmacy.com complies with these guidelines by:

- Publishing this affiliate disclosure on a dedicated, accessible page
- Including this disclosure link in the site footer, visible on every page
- Marking affiliate links with `rel="sponsored"` attributes in the site code
- Not allowing affiliate relationships to influence pharmacy search results, which are based solely on proximity and availability

## How to Identify Affiliate Links

Affiliate links on 24HourPharmacy.com are managed through ThirstyAffiliates, a link management plugin. All affiliate links use cloaked `/go/` URLs — for example:

- `/go/goodrx/`
- `/go/singlecare/`
- `/go/amazon-pharmacy/`

If a link on this site follows the `/go/[partner]/` URL format, it is an affiliate link. These links are tracked so that 24HourPharmacy.com can receive credit for referrals. The tracking is handled by cookies set by our affiliate partners — see our Privacy Policy for details on how these cookies are used.

## Our Editorial Standards

24HourPharmacy.com's affiliate relationships do not influence the editorial content or the results returned by our pharmacy finder tool.

- **Pharmacy finder results** are based on real-time proximity data from the Google Places API. Pharmacies are not ranked or filtered based on whether they have an affiliate relationship with us.
- **Discount card recommendations** are based on acceptance rates at major pharmacy chains and documented savings potential — not on commission rates.
- **Content coverage** is driven by user need and search demand, not by partner requests or paid placement.

We will not recommend a product or service we would not use ourselves or that we believe provides poor value to users. If our affiliate partners change their programs in ways that conflict with user benefit, we will update or remove those recommendations.
