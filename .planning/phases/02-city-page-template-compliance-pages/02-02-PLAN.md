Write 600-800 words for the /privacy-policy/ WordPress page. This is the most detailed compliance page. Structure with these H2 sections:
- "Information We Collect" — 24HourPharmacy.com does NOT require user accounts or collect personal information directly. We do not store names, emails, addresses, or health information. We collect anonymous usage data through the analytics tools described below.
- "Analytics and Tracking" — Describe each tool specifically:
  - **Google Analytics 4 (GA4):** Collects anonymous usage data including pages visited, time on site, referral source, device type, and approximate location (city-level). GA4 uses first-party cookies. Data is processed by Google LLC.
  - **Microsoft Clarity:** Records anonymous session replays and heatmaps to understand how users interact with the site. Clarity does not collect personal information. Data is processed by Microsoft Corporation.
  - **CookieYes:** Manages the cookie consent banner on this site. CookieYes records your consent preferences. You can change your preferences at any time using the "Manage Preferences" link in the site footer.
- "Cookies" — Types of cookies used: Strictly Necessary (CookieYes consent), Analytics (GA4, Clarity), Advertising (affiliate tracking cookies from GoodRx, SingleCare, Amazon Associates, CJ Affiliate, Impact). Analytics and advertising cookies are only set after consent via CookieYes banner.
- "Affiliate Link Tracking" — When you click an affiliate link (e.g., /go/goodrx/), the affiliate partner may set cookies on your device to track the referral. These cookies are governed by the partner's privacy policy, not ours.
- "Google Maps / Places API" — The pharmacy finder widget uses Google Maps Platform. When you use the finder, Google may collect your approximate location (with your permission) and usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
- "Children's Privacy" — This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
- "Your Rights" — California residents: under CCPA, you may opt out of the sale of personal information. Use the CookieYes banner "Do Not Sell My Information" option. We do not sell personal information, but affiliate cookies may constitute a "sale" under CCPA.
- "Changes to This Policy" — Last updated: March 2026.
